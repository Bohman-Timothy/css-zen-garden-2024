Theme: Sea Depths

The theme is based on the idea that the background view goes deeper into the ocean as you scroll down. It starts above water and includes a sun, clouds, and a boat, and there are two layers of water featuring a fish and air bubbles, and a starfish and an underwater sand castle are on the sandy ocean floor.

Designed by Timothy Bohman, Ella Galbraith (former team member), and Kevin Kirby for the Advanced CSS course (WDD 331R) in the Spring 2024 semester at BYU-Idaho.

Here is a link to the source SCSS files for the style guide and theme.
